Privacy Policy for NuvyLynx

Last updated: May 22, 2025

NuvyLynx is a social platform designed to connect users by sharing advice and insights. This Privacy Policy explains how NuvyLynx, developed and owned by Alessandro Pitocchi (“we”, “us”, or “our”), collects, uses, and protects your Personal Data when you register, post content, interact with other users, or browse the service.

1. Data Controller

Alessandro Pitocchi
Email: support@vicelinx.com

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Registration: Name, username, email address, password, profile photo, bio.
  • User Content: Posts, comments, messages, feedback, ratings, and any media you upload.
  • Settings & Preferences: Communication preferences, notification settings, and language choices.
  • Contact Information: Email and other contact details you submit for support or notifications.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, session duration, and interaction events.
  • Device & Technical Data: Device model, operating system, browser type, IP address, and device identifiers.
  • Location Data: Approximate location derived from IP address (if you grant permission).
  • Analytics & Cookies: Cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to analyze site usage and improve experience.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To create and manage your account, and provide the social advice service.
  • To display your profile and content to other users as you choose to share.
  • To send notifications, messages, and updates relevant to your activity and preferences.
  • To moderate content, detect policy violations, and maintain a safe community.
  • To analyze usage patterns, improve features, and optimize performance.
  • To respond to support requests, feedback, and inquiries.
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect our rights.

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

  • Consent: When you provide personal data and agree to this policy.
  • Contract: Processing necessary to perform our service agreement (account creation, content delivery).
  • Legitimate Interests: For security, fraud prevention, and service improvements.

5. Sharing & Disclosure

  • Public Sharing: Content you post publicly is viewable by other users and third-party services if you enable integrations.
  • Direct Messages: Private messages are encrypted in transit and stored securely; accessible only by the sender and recipient.
  • Service Providers: Third-party analytics, hosting, email, and crash-reporting services, under appropriate confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose data to comply with legal processes or protect rights, safety, and property.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account data and content for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, your personal data will be removed within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational measures, including encryption in transit (HTTPS) and at rest, access controls, and regular security audits to protect your data.

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in servers located within or outside the EEA. We ensure transfers comply with applicable safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, delete, restrict, or port your personal data, as well as withdraw consent. You can manage your data via account settings or by contacting us at support@vicelinx.com.

10. Children’s Privacy

NuvyLynx is not intended for use by children under 16. If you are between 16 and 18 years old, you must have permission from a parent or guardian to use NuvyLynx We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 16; if we become aware that we have inadvertently collected such data, we will delete it promptly.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. We will notify you of material changes via email or an in-app notice.
